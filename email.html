MN Private Colleges Fall Fair

By attending this virtual recruiting fair, candidates will have the opportunity to network with Minnesota, regional, and national companies hiring in the areas of consulting, banking/finance, actuarial science, stats/data science, technology, business analysis, management and leadership training/development, and project management. We welcome sophomores, juniors, seniors and May/Summer 2020 undergraduate alumni from all majors to register for the fair.

For more information visit the Career Center
